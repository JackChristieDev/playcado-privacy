Privacy Policy for Playcado

Last updated: January 8, 2026

1. Introduction

Playcado ("we," "our," or "us") is a client application for the Jellyfin media server. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This application is designed to connect to your own personal Jellyfin server. We do not host your content, nor do we have access to your personal media library.

2. Data Collection and Usage

We do not collect, store, or share your personal information, viewing history, or media library content on our servers. All media data is transmitted directly between your device and your personal Jellyfin server.

3. Application Permissions

To function correctly, Playcado requires specific permissions:

  • Local Network (iOS) / Nearby Devices (Android): Required solely to discover and connect to Google Cast™ devices (Chromecast, Google TV) on your network. We do not scan your network for any other purpose.
  • Location (Android): Android versions prior to 12 require Location permission to discover Bluetooth/WiFi devices for Casting. We do not track, store, or share your physical location.
  • Notifications: Used solely to display the progress of active downloads in the status bar.

4. Data Storage

  • Authentication: Your server address, username, and password/token are stored securely on your device using the device's native secure storage (Keychain on iOS, Keystore on Android). This data is never sent to us.
  • Downloads: Media files you download are stored locally on your device for offline playback.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Sentry: We use Sentry to collect anonymous crash reports and performance data. This helps us fix bugs and improve the app. This data does not include your personal media content or server credentials.
  • Google Cast SDK: Used to enable casting functionality.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
